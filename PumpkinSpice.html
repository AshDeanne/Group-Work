Pumpkin Spice Latte

Pumpkin Spice Latte

The ultimate autumn icon! This modern classic is beautifully spiced and creamy, and warms you down to your boots. Here's how to get your hands wrapped around a mug of your own. This recipe is naturally sweetened, vegan and dairy-free, we use oat milk but feel free to substitute your own dairy/non-dairy option.

prep time

5 min

cook time

5 min

serving

1 serving

Instructions

Step 1

Put the pumpkin pur√©e in a large heatproof glass or mug. Stir in the spices and espresso or strong coffee.

Step 2

Heat the milk in a saucepan over a low heat until steaming and frothy.

Step 3

Pour into the glass or mug, and spoon over any froth.

Step 4

Stir to combine, then dust with more cinnamon or some pumpkin spice before serving.
